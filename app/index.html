Making a Worldwide Impact with Outerworldly Coffee

As the second largest opium producer and the largest methamphetamine producer, Myanmar is an epicentre of drug activity. After a military coup, US sanctions, and a crumbling banking system, these farmers now face the difficult decision between growing opium or foregoing their livelihood.

That’s why at Morning Rocket, we offer an alternative for farmers

By sourcing our coffee from the Shan State, the region where 90% of Myanmar’s opium is grown, we are changing lives one brew at a time.

Our Mission

We are committed to transforming livelihoods by empowering local farmers in Myanmar to grow specialty coffee instead of methamphetamine or opium.
